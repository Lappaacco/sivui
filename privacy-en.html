3. Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Booking and customer service: Processing bookings received through the booking system (Vello) and customer service (legal basis: performance of contract)

  • Web analytics: Monitoring and improving website usage (legal basis: legitimate interest)

4. Register Data Content

The following information may be stored in the register:

Booking (Vello):

  • Name

  • Booked appointments and services
  • Any additional information (provided by customer when booking)

Contact form (Web3Forms):

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message
  • IP address (automatically stored)
  • Timestamp

5. Regular Data Sources

Personal data is collected from:

  • The customer themselves (booking, contact form)
  • Automatically from website usage (cookies, analytics)

6. Data Disclosure and Transfers

Data is not disclosed to third parties, except:

  • Vello Oy: Booking system provider (Finland, EU)

  • Google Maps: Map service (Google LLC, USA - data transfer based on EU-approved standard clauses)
  • Legal authorities: When required by law in legally mandated situations

9. Rights of the Data Subject

You have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Right to check what data about you has been stored
  • Right to rectification: Right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Right to request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing: Right to request restriction of processing your data
  • Right to object: Right to object to processing of your data based on legitimate interest
  • Right to data portability: Right to receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to withdraw consent: Right to withdraw your given consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint: Right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority (Data Protection Ombudsman's Office, tietosuoja.fi)

10. Data Security

Personal data is protected with appropriate technical and organizational measures:

  • The website uses HTTPS encryption
  • Data is stored on secure servers
  • Access to data is restricted to only necessary personnel
  • We only use GDPR-compliant service providers
